## Add IBEX Apps to VHD

1. Mount IBEX VHD
   1. Open `cmd` in `O3` mode
   1. `diskmgmt.msc`
   1. Click Action -> Attach VHD
       1. I had to click help first
   1. Select location of VHD
1. Open mapped drive copy the following:
    1. Copy EPICS
    1. Copy Client
    1. Copy EPICS UTILS
    1. Copy Python
    1. Copy ICP Binaries into EPICS
    1. Copy MySql
