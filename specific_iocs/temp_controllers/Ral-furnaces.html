RAL Furnaces

RAL Furnace 3

RAL furnace 3 is a composite device, part of which is a Turbotronik NT 10 talking to a Eurotherm, the other part is a Pfeiffer vacuum gauge That vacuum gauge is a TPG26X_01 referred to on both GEM (furnace pressure component) and POLARIS (Eurotherms with barotrons component).
