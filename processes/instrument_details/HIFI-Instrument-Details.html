HIFI

Warning

These instrument details pages provide historical context about the devices on a specific instrument, at the time they were migrated to IBEX. These pages may not be up to date with the latest instrument configuration.

This page collects information that will be useful for the implementation of the IBEX control system on HIFI.

Background & Timeline

HIFI is high magnetic-field muon instrument at ISIS, on TS1. The HIFI web page describes the background to the instrument.

Note: Motion

There is a project relating to the motion on the South Side Muons in progress, this information may be out of date

Note: Jaws

Provide information about HIFI jaws.

Note: Vacuum System

TPG300 support is implemented via #216 and #2063

Note: Eurotherm

Eurotherms are used to control temperature Orange Cryostat, CCR and Furnace devices.

Note: Aeroflex/IFR

Aeroflex has been through a complicated series of sales in recent years. The Aeroflex manuals are probably a good place to start, along with any existing VIs. See also IFR 2023A.

There appears to be no driver for the Aeroflex 2023A in C:\LabVIEW Modules\. However, there is a driver for an Aeroflex 2030 in C:\LabVIEW Modules\Drivers\Aeroflex 2030.
